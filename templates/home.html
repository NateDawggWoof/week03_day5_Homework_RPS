RvSvP

Rules

The game is played by 2 players who each secretly choose one of 3 objects, rock, paper or scissors.
Simutenasiouly they reveal their choices at the count of 3.

The outcome of the game is determined by 3 simple rules:
Rock wins against scissors.
Scissors win against paper.
Paper wins against Rock

if both players make the same choice the game is a tie.

How to play with web App

There are 2 ways to play RvSvP. There's PvP, 2 human players or PvC playing against a computer.

To play pvp ente rin the url /player1name/player1choice/player2name/player2choice i.e /nath/paper/steve/rock
